Biography

Guo-Hua Wang received his Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology of Nanjing University in May 2023, and he was a member of LAMDA Group. Before his Ph.D. career, he received the B.Sc. degree in School of Management & Engineering in June 2018 from Nanjing University. In the same year, he was admitted to study for the Ph.D. degree in Nanjing University without entrance examination.

  • In July to Novemeber 2021, he was an intern at YouTu Lab, Tencent.

  • In March to October 2022, he was an intern at Media Computing, MSRA, Microsoft.

Research Interests

His research interests include Machine Learning and Deep Learning. Currently, he focuses on:

  • Multi-model Learning: Knowledge Distillation, Model Compression/Acceleration.

  • Few-label Learning: Semi-Supervised Learning, Few-shot Learning.
